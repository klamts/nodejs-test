Practice -gh Pronunciation

How gh is pronounced

  • Silent "gh": No sound at all. (e.g., high, night)
  • "gh" = /f/: Pronounced like "f". (e.g., laugh, cough, rough)
  • "gh" = /g/: Pronounced like "g". (less common, e.g., ghost, ghastly)
